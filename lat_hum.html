Latitude vs. Humidity



Observations


On 01/05/2017, humidity was consistently higher in the tropics, and lower north of the Tropic of Cancer, and south of the Tropic of Capricorn. There appears to be some correlation between coastal locations and humidity; humidity appears to be higher in coastal locations, and lower inland.
